Frequently Asked Questions

How do I upload my assignment?

Login as a student, go to your dashboard, and click "Upload" next to the assignment.

Can I edit my submission before the deadline?

Yes, you can update your submission anytime before the deadline.

What if I miss the deadline?

You will not be able to submit late unless your teacher enables late submission.
